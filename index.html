About Me

Over the years, I’ve honed my problem-solving and technical skills through projects involving medical equipment installation, from X-rays to laboratory systems.

I’m proficient in Excel, SPSS, Python, and Power BI, and I’m constantly refining my skills in database management and responsive web design. With a strong foundation in data science, I’m excited to explore machine learning as my next frontier.
